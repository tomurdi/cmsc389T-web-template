Form Verification

College Success Scholars, also known as CSS, is a program that helps Black and Latino Males academically, socially, and professionally throughout their time in college. Being part of this program, a group of 5, including me, volunteered to revamp our existing database. One of the projects that I was assigned was to validate forms between mentors and mentees. For context, mentors are anyone on the leadership team and mentees are the opposite. These mentors check up on their mentees once a week to see how their doing in school and mentally as well. Both the mentee and mentor should submit some sort of Google Form saying that they met and where they met. Sometimes, mentors or mentees could lie about whether they met or not or how they met. My project was to check our database, scan over 200+ forms, and create some sort of mapping to the mentor's form and their mentees' form. Next, I could check to see whether or not they actually met.
